Roast Beef

Roast beef is a classic main dish that is perfect for special occasions or Sunday dinners. This recipe creates a tender and flavorful roast beef with a delicious herb crust.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef roast (such as ribeye or sirloin)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375째F.
  2. Place the beef roast in a roasting pan and rub it with the olive oil, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, rosemary, and thyme, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  3. Roast the beef in the preheated oven for approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches your desired level of doneness (135째F for medium-rare, 145째F for medium, or 160째F for well done).
  4. Remove the roast from the oven and let it rest for 15 minutes before carving.
  5. Slice the roast beef and serve it with your favorite sides.
