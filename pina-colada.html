Pi単a Colada

The Pi単a Colada is a classic tropical cocktail that combines the flavors of pineapple, coconut, and rum. It's a refreshing and creamy drink perfect for summer parties or relaxing by the beach.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces white rum
  • 4 ounces pineapple juice
  • 2 ounces coconut cream
  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • Pineapple slices and maraschino cherries for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the white rum, pineapple juice, coconut cream, and crushed ice.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the mixture into a glass.
  4. Garnish with a pineapple slice and a maraschino cherry.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!
